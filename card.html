Cat

Cats have an extra organ that allows them to taste scents on the air, which is why your cat stares at you with her mouth open from time to time

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus linn
  • Average length: 30 inches
  • Label: 14 years
  • Habitat: Areas of human habitation.

Felis catus most likely originated from African wild cats or Asian desert cats. Although both species have the same number of chromosomes as Felis catus, Asian desert cats are common around human settlements and are easily tamed. There are over 100 breeds of domestic cats but all have a very similar body shape and size.
